How to use the snow wax?


Rub on the desired amount of JWAX snow wax on your snowboard or skis. You will need more wax if the snow is fresh or rough. One quick fix waxing will last about 2-3 runs, depending on the track length and snow conditions before you notice a slowdown. If used on street or snow parks you can even wax the rails for those smoothest slides. Waxing a snowboard and skis has never been so easy and fun. You can also hot iron when at home or at the service station.

If You notice that you are losing speed or even stopping way before your snow buddies, especially on more even surface it’s worth to give our wax a try so you don’t get stuck in the snow. Note that it works best in warmer temperatures above -5˚C/23˚F.
