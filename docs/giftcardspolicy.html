Gift cards policy



Validity:

The Gift Card is valid for a period of one (1) year from the date of purchase.

Usage:

The Gift Card is valid for a single purchase only. Any remaining balance after the purchase + delivery will be forfeited and not carried forward for future purchases. Any amount above the gift card value will be charged additionally as in a standard purchase. E.g. Your gift card value is 50 eur and your final order amount is 55 eur then you should pay 5 euros at checkout.

Redemption:

The Gift Card can be redeemed for products on our website within the specified validity period.

Delivery:

Upon completion of the purchase, the customer will receive an email containing the Gift Card details. Please ensure that the provided email address is accurate to receive this information.

Transferable:

The Gift Card is transferable and can be used by an individual different from the name associated with the purchase.

Refunds:

Gift Card purchases are non-refundable.

Lost or Stolen Cards or emails:

We are not responsible for lost or stolen Gift Cards. Please treat your Gift Card like cash.

Combination with Other Offers:

Gift Cards cannot be combined with other promotional offers or discounts.

Cancellation:

We reserve the right to cancel any Gift Card if we believe it was obtained fraudulently or used in violation of these terms.

For any questions or concerns regarding Gift Cards, please drop us a line. By purchasing and using the Gift Card, you agree to these terms and conditions.
