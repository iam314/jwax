Who is this for?


JWAX fits perfect in the hands of: Street and Freestyle skiers and snowboarders. People who prefer on the spot quick fix. Or those who are not servicing or waxing their equipment regularly. Snow enthusiasts with different sets of skies and snowboards for different conditions. People who want to have fun with a snow wax of the finest quality.

Anything else?


You can also hot iron it. Will last longer on the ski or snowboard base. HINT: Always apply snow wax on your skis or snowboard base after the winter season to keep your equipment in good shape for the next one.

How to use the snow wax?


Rub on the desired amount of JWAX snowboard and ski wax to cover your base. You will need more wax if the snow is fresh or rough. One quick fix waxing will last about 2-3 runs depending on the track length and snow conditions before you notice a slowdown. If used on street or snow parks you can even wax the rails for those smoothest slides. Waxing a snowboard and skis has never been so easy and fun. You can also hot iron snow wax when at home or at the service station.

When to use snow wax?


If You notice that you are losing speed or even stopping way before your snow buddies, especially on more even surface it’s worth to give our wax a try so you don’t get stuck in the snow. Note that it works best in warmer temperatures above -5˚C/23˚F.

How big it is?
